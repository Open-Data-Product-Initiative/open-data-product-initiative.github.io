Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

We typically respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. For technical questions or issues reported on GitHub, our team aims to provide an initial response within 24 hours.

The best way to report technical issues is through our GitHub repositories. Please submit an issue to the appropriate version repository with detailed information about the bug, including steps to reproduce, expected behavior, and your environment.

While ODPS is designed to be extensible and adaptable to various needs, we encourage organizations to work within the standard framework. If you have specific requirements, please contact us to discuss how they might be incorporated into future versions or implemented as extensions.

We welcome contributions from the community! You can contribute by submitting pull requests on GitHub, participating in discussions, sharing use cases, or joining the Technical Steering Committee. Visit our Development page for more information.

Yes, we maintain several channels for community interaction. You can join discussions on GitHub issues, participate in our monthly community calls, or connect with other users through our LinkedIn group. Details are available on our Development page.
